About Us

The website is for sharing Malaysian food recipes. It wants to be a go-to place for people into Malaysian food, letting them explore, learn, and try out traditional and modern dishes. Our aim is to make everyone love Malaysian food and appreciate the special techniques that make this culinary tradition unique. This website shares recipes, especially traditional ones from each state in Malaysia. We want to make sure these recipes are not forgotten in today's world. In modern times, many traditional foods have changed, and we want to keep the original recipes alive by showing people how to make them. The website also gives clear and simple recipes for different Malaysian dishes.
